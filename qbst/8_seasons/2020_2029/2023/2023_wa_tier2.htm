First-grade teams are listed in bold.

For the NPL and the State League, the championship was awarded to the team finishing on top of the table at the end of the regular season.

The Top Four Cup was a separate final series played after the regular season for the NPL.

There was no Top Four Cup final series conducted in WA State League 1 and 2 this season.
