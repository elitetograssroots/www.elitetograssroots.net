Note 1: The original draw for the Charity Cup semi-finals was: Dinmore Bush Rats vs. South Brisbane at Dinmore; North Brisbane vs. Blackstone Rovers at Brisbane (Brisbane Telegraph 16/09/1899).

The final was to have been played 14/10/1899 (Brisbane Telegraph 16/09/1899), but Dinmore Bush Rats had already won the Charity Cup earlier (Qld Times 12/10/1899).

Note 2: The venue for the Dinmore Bush Rats vs. South Brisbane match on 07/10/1899 was the Rugby Union Ground, Bowen Bridge Road (Brisbane Telegraph 07/10/1899). This match was also described as a friendly match (Brisbane Courier 06/10/1899).

Note 3: Dinmore Bush Rats had won the Charity Cup (Qld Times 12/10/1899) meaning the match on 07/10/1899 must have been the final. The result of the final match was not stated.
