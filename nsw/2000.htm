Sydney AFL

First Grade -- Season 2000

 

Season notes

Sydney Swans Reserves competed in the Sydney AFL competition for this season only.

Baulkham Hills formed a partnership with the West Coast Eagles in early 2000 and was renamed East Coast Eagles.

East Sydney and University of NSW amalgamated after the 1999 season to form UNSW-Eastern Suburbs.
