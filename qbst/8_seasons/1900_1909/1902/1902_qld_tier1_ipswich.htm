Final season tables -- West Moreton British Football Association 1902

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

The senior premiership consisted of only two teams and three rounds.

The junior competition was the most active league in the region in 1902 and included one Brisbane team (Eskgrove).

Note 1: At the WMBFA annual general meeting (Qld Times 28/02/1903) it was reported that three senior teams competed in 1902. However, contemporary reports indicate only Dinmore Bush Rats and Bundamba Rangers contested the senior premiership, in a series of three matches.

Note 2: Although there were no references to Ipswich Royals being the third senior team in 1902, the club won the 1901 Junior premiership and would have been eligible to be promoted to senior status.

It was reported at the WMBFA AGM on 25/02/1903 that there were three senior teams competing in the first-grade competition in 1902. (Qld Times 28/02/1903)
