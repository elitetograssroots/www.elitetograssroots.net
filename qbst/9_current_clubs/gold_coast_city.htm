Notes:

Palm Beach Soccer Club was invited to field a team in the Brisbane Premier League (BPL) in 1998 and played as Gold Coast City FC from 1998 to 2002 while continuing to field a team in the Gold Coast League.

The team in the BPL played as Palm Beach from 2003 to 2007 after which the team withdrew from the BPLdue to financial reasons.

In 2013 Palm Beach SC entered a team in the new Queensland NPL, again playing as Gold Coast City. After the 2017 season Palm Beach SC withdrew financial support for the team and its NPL licence was not renewed.
