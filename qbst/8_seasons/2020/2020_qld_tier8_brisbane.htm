Final season tables -- Football Brisbane -- City League -- 2020

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

The 2020 season was suspended in March due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Earlier results were annulled and the season recommenced in July in a condensed format and with the list of teams in each division revised.

No final series were conducted by Football Brisbane for this season.
