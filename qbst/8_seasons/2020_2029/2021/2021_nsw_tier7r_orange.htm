Final season tables -- Orange & District Football Association 2021

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

Orange & District FA points system for 2021: 4 points for a win or a bye, 2 points for a draw, 1 point for a loss.

On 9 September 2021 the Orange & District FA announced that its 2021 season was cancelled.

No premiers or champions were declared.
