Sydney AFL

First Grade -- Season 1999

 

Season notes

Campbelltown returned to Sydney AFL this season, after competing in the ACTAFL for three seasons (1996 to 1998).

Baulkham Hills formed a partnership with the West Coast Eagles after this season and was renamed East Coast Eagles.

East Sydney amalgamated with University of NSW after this season to form UNSW-Eastern Suburbs.
