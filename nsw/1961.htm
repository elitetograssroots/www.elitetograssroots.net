New South Wales Australian National Football League

Season 1961

 

Season notes

Commencing this season, teams consisted of 18 players per side, 16 on the field and a 17th and 18th man as replacements as required.

Preliminary Final

03/09/1961 (Sun) Newtown 13-10-88 Sydney Naval 14-10-94 Venue: Trumper Park

Notes on the Preliminary Final:

When the siren sounded to end the final quarter, the teams were level. A goal after the siren made Sydney Naval the winners.

Newtown protested on the basis that the timekeepers had erred in not continuously sounding the siren indicating the conclusion of the game, plus the first quarter ended six minutes short of the regulation 25 minutes.

The protest was upheld and the match ordered to be replayed. (Source: The Australian National Football Record 9 September 1961)
