Final season tables -- Football South Coast 2022

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

Due to record rainfall in the Illawarra District in 2022, the format of the competition was revised in early August 2022.

For all divisions except the District League First Grade, the ranking of teams was decided on a Points per Game ratio.

There were no final series held in the divisions of the Community League in 2022.
