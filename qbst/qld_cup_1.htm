From 2008 to 2021 only clubs competing in Football Brisbane competitions were eligible to enter the Canale Cup, except in 2014 when it also served as a qualifying competition for the Australia Cup. This meant:

---- From 2008 to 2012, Queensland State League (Tier 2) clubs were ineligible to enter.

---- In 2013, and from 2015 to 2021, Queensland National Premier League (Tier 2) clubs were ineligible to enter.

---- In 2019, Football Queensland Premier League 1 (Tier 3) clubs were also ineligible to enter.

---- In 2021, Football Queensland Premier League 2 (Tier 4) clubs were also ineligible to enter.
