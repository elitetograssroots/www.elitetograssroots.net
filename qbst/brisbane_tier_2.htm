Brisbane Premiership Top Four

Tier 2 -- 1921 to 2018 (94 seasons)

 

The List | Rankings (1921 to 2016)

 

This listing covers the second tier in Brisbane football:

  • Division B -- 1921 to 1922
  • Division 2 -- 1923 to 1961 (except 1930 to 1933 and 1942 to 1945)
  • QSF Division 1 -- 1962 to 1964
  • Division 2 -- 1965 to 1982
  • Division 1 -- 1983
  • Intermediate League -- 1984 to 1986
  • Division 2 -- 1987 to 1996
  • Semi Pro Division [North and South divisions] -- 1997 to 2000 (two premiers each season)
  • Semi Pro Division [single division] -- 2001
  • Division 1 -- 2002
  • Premier Division 1 -- 2003 to 2012
  • Capital League 1 -- 2013 to 2018

Notes:

The list above refers to the final premiership standings:

  • Finals series played irregularly during the 1920s have been factored in, as these decided the premier team.
  • Final series played every season since 1959 (except 2000) are not factored in, as these decided the champions, a separate honour.

 

Reserves teams achieved top four rankings as follows:

  • Multiple instances between 1934 and 1941
  • Brisbane Lions (1984)
  • Brisbane City (1985).

 

The following rankings remain to be conclusively established through future research:

  • 1995 -- Nerang and Beenleigh came first and second, probably in that order. St Helens United beat Nerang in the Grand Final, and must have finished third or fourth
  • 1975 -- Fourth place went to either Mitchelton or Redcliffe City
  • 1974 -- Southside Eagles almost certainly finished premiers ahead of St Helens United
  • 1964 -- Hollandia-Inala beat Polonia in the Grand Final, so Polonia finished in the top four.
