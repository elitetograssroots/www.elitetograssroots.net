New South Wales Australian National Football League

Season 1946

 

Season notes

North Shore returned to the league this season.

R.A.A.F. left the league after the 1945 season but rejoined in 1947.

There were no designated home grounds this season because all fixtures were played at Trumper Park or Erskineville Oval.
