Final season tables -- Football Victoria 2019 -- State League 3

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

No final series were conducted in the FV State League for this season. However, the champions of each regional league played off for the divisional title.

There was no relegation from State League 3 due to Football Victoria's 2020 restructure.
