Sydney Football League

First Grade -- Season 1993

 

Season notes

Baulkham Hills was promoted to the SFL this season after finishing top of the 1992 SFA Division One ladder (but losing both matches in the final series).

The Sydney Football Association (SFA) effectively functioned as a second division of the SFL.
