Note 1: Blackstone Rovers was suspended by the QFA on 12/07/1921 after walking off during a Tristram Shield game. The QFA heard an appeal on 30/08/1921 at which the suspension was lifted and replaced by a deduction of six points.

Note 2: Round 10 Bulimba Rangers vs. Blackstone Rovers was cancelled on 11/06/1921 due to an unfit ground, rescheduled for 10/09/1921, the forfeited by Bulimba Rangers.

Note 3: Round 18 Toowong vs. Blackstone Rovers remained unplayed when Blackstone was re-instated. The game was not scheduled for 17/09/1921 (when the premiership play-off occurred) and therefore must have been forfeited by Toowong.

Note 4: Round 18 Queen's Park vs. Western Suburbs was- re-scheduled to 03/09/1921. The result was a draw, score unknown (Daily Mail 05/09/1921).

 

Division 2 Premiership Play-off: Blackstone Rovers 1 Queen's Park 0 (after a 2-2 draw)
