Founded in 1960 as Mountain Rangers, the club was renamed Mount Gravatt in 1962 and operated solely as a junior club until the end of 1964.

It absorbed the Excelsior Soccer Club (based in Stones Corner) when it commenced in the senior ranks in 1965.
