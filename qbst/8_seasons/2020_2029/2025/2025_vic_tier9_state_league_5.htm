Final season tables -- Football Victoria 2025 -- State League 5

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

The four zones of State League 5 were re-structured into two new divisions in 2026: State League 6 and State League 7, each containing two zones.

Similarly, the four zones of State League 4 were re-structured into two new divisions in 2026: State League 4 and State League 5, each containing two zones.

In each State League 5 division below, teams 3-6 join the new State League 6 in 2026, teams 7-12 join the new State League 7 in 2026.
