Sydney Football League

First Grade -- Season 1986

 

Season notes

After this season, Bankstown Sports was not admitted to the new NSW State League in 1987, instead competing in the second tier SFL Division One (source: Record 4-4-1987, page 17).

Newtown folded after failing to meet the criteria for admission to the 1987 NSW State League (source: Record 4-4-1987, page 5).
