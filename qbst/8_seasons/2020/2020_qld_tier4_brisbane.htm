Final season tables -- Football Brisbane 2020

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

The 2020 season was suspended in March due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Earlier results were annulled and the season recommenced in July in a condensed format.

In the condensed format, each division completed a round-robin Stage One. Teams 1 to 6 progressed to the Premier Division; Teams 7 to 12 competed in Plate Division. Reserves groupings and fixtures for Stage Two were based on Stage One standings in First Grade.

There were no final series conducted by Football Brisbane for this season.
