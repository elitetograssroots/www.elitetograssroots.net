Note: In total there was a total of 66 extra goals between two divisions because in all 22 rounds a team had the bye.

All teams in the Illawarra Credit Union District League received 3 points + 3 goals added for one bye in the inter-division matches.

All teams in the Peoplecare District League received 6 points + 6 goals added for two byes in the matches within their division.

Teams played twice against the teams in their division (14 of the 22 rounds) and once against the teams in the other division.
