Final season tables -- West Moreton British Football Association 1911

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

 

Three Ipswich clubs competed in the senior QBFA Challenge Cup in 1911.

Two of these clubs, Dinmore Bush Rats and Bundamba Rangers also fielded reserve teams in the QBFA First Grade Junior competition.

WMBFA commenced a Junior Minor Grade competition on 10 June 1911 (Qld Times 03/06/1911), initially with five teams, and effectively a third-grade competition.

The teams that are known to have entered these junior grade competitions are listed below
