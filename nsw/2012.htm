AFL Sydney

Premier Division -- Season 2012

 

Season notes

East Coast Eagles was renamed Sydney Hills Eagles and known under this name from 2012 to 2014.

From 2012 to 2014, Sydney Hills Eagles fielded their first-grade team in the North East Australian Football League (NEAFL) and their reserves team in the AFL Sydney Premier Division.

From 2012 to 2019, Sydney University fielded their first-grade team in the NEAFL and their reserves team in the AFL Sydney Premier Division.
