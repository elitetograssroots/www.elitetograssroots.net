Brisbane Division 1

 

 

 

In 1962 there was a split between the Queensland Soccer Football Association and the newly founded Queensland Soccer Federation.

It is probable YMCA continued in 1962 with the Association (list of Association teams for that season currently unknown) and was renamed Triangles Soccer Club.

Triangles competed in Division 2 of the Association in 1963 and was absorbed by South Coast United in 1965.
