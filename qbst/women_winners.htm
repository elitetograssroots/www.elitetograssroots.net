Divisions covered in these lists and rankings

Tier 1 covers the following divisions:

  • South Qld Womens Soccer Association - Division 1 - 1976 to 1996
  • Brisbane Womens Soccer Association - Premier League - 1997 to 2006
  • Football Brisbane - Premier League - 2007 to 2011
  • Football Brisbane - SEQ Diamond League - 2012 and 2013
  • Football Brisbane - SEQ Premier League - 2014
  • Football Queensland - NPL Women - 2015 to 2020

Tier 2 covers the following divisions:

  • South Qld Womens Soccer Association - Division 2 - 1976 to 1996
  • Brisbane Womens Soccer Association - Division 1 - 1997 to 2006
  • Football Brisbane - Division 1 - 2007 to 2011
  • Football Brisbane - Sapphire League - 2012 and 2013
  • Football Brisbane - Premier League - 2014 to 2020
