Note 1: Abbreviations in the results for matches where the result is known, but the score is unavailable: W = Won; D = Drew; L = Lost; U = No record of result or if the match was played.

Note 2: Inferred result from the points table in The Referee (Sydney) 29 June 1910. Dinmore Bush Rats scored a total of 7 goals in their Round 2 & 6 fixtures combined. Assumed to be 6-0 and 1-0 results respectively.

Note 3: Inferred result from the points table in The Referee (Sydney) 29 June 1910. Woodville scored a total of 4 goals in their Round 2 & 6 fixtures combined. Assumed to be 1-6 and 3-4 results respectively.

Note 4: Round 4 28/05/1910 Bulimba Rangers vs. Valley was abandoned after Bulimba Rangers had a player sent off for ungentlemanly conduct and spectator encroached stopping further play. Bulimba Rangers was also going to protest against Valley's second goal. The points table in The Referee (Sydney) 29 June 1910 indicates that the result stood.

Note 5: Round 5 04/06/1910 Eskgrove vs. Valley. The result was given as a 2-1 win to Eskgrove (Brisbane Courier 6 June 1910). However, the halfway table published in The Referee (Sydney) 29 June 1910 indicates that Valley and Eskgrove drew. The leading points scorers in The Referee (Sydney) 27 July 1910 has Eskgrove with 12 points after Round 11, further indicating a draw. However, the postponed Round 8 Bulimba Rangers vs. Dinmore Bush Rats was played on 13/08/1910, indicating Eskgrove was on 18 points at this stage, and Dinmore Bush Rats still had to play to become premiers. Therefore, the result appears as a 2-1 win to Eskgrove after weighing up the evidence. However, it should be further noted that in determining other results using the halfway table (Referee 29/06/1910), a 2-2 draw has been assumed.

Note 6: Round 12 23/07/1910 Wellington vs. Dinmore Bush Rats was abandoned after Wellington walked off after a penalty was awarded to Dinmore Bush Rats (which was then taken and converted) making the score 1-1. Spectators encroached onto the field preventing further play. At the QBFA Meeting 26/07/1910, the match was awarded to Dinmore Bush Rats.

Note 7: Round 14 06/08/1910 Eskgrove vs. Woodville. It can be inferred that Eskgrove won or received a forfeit because otherwise Dinmore Bush Rats would not have needed to play their postponed Round 8 fixture against Bulimba Rangers on 13/08/1910to claim the premiership.
