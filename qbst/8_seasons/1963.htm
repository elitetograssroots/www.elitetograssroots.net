QSF - B&ISFA Unity Series

The QSF was formed at the start of the 1962 season. Many clubs had remained with the Association which had continued for two seasons (1962 and 1963) before succumbing to a merger with the QSF.

The QSF State League was reducing to 8 clubs in 1964, so a 'Unity Series' was held to determine the 8th place.

Qualifying for this series were the bottom three clubs in this season's QSF State League, the winner of QSF Division 1, and the top four clubs in the Association.
