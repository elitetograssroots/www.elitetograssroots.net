Final season tables -- Southern Districts Soccer Football Association 2024

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

The Premier League was divided into two divisions in 2024. Seven teams fielded teams in both divisions. There was no Premier League Reserves division in 2024.

After this season, the top two senior divisions were restructured into the Premier League (single division) and the Championship (a new division).
