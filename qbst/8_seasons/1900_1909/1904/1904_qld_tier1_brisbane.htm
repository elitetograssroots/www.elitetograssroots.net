Note 1: Abbreviations in the results for matches where the result is known, but the score is unavailable: W = Won; D = Drew; L = Lost; U = No record of result or if the match was played.

Note 2: The original Round One fixtures included Reliance vs. Rocklea with Dinmore Bush Rats receiving the bye. This was amended after Rocklea withdrew.

Note 3: A protest by Markets against the Dinmore goal was dismissed (Qld Times 07/06/1904).

Note 4: The semi-final fixtures were decided by a draw that draw occurred on 22/07/1904 (Brisbane Courier 18/07/1904).

Note 5: The semi-final first replay Reliance vs. Blackstone Rovers B was abandoned after 70 minutes by the referee due to spectator encroachment (Qld Times 06/08/1904).
