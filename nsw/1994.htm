Sydney Football League

First Grade -- Season 1994

 

Season notes

Balmain was promoted to the SFL this season after going undefeated in the 1993 regular season of the SFA Division One (but losing to University of NSW twice in the final series).

Holroyd-Parramatta merged with Blacktown (SFA) after the 1994 season and the merged team competed in the SFA in 1995.

Sydney University left the SFL after the 1994 season to join the SFA in 1995.

The Sydney Football Association (SFA) effectively functioned as a second division of the SFL.

Football Park, Macquarie Fields was renamed Monarch Oval after this season.
