Club formed in 2005 by the affiliation of St Joseph's Junior Soccer Club (formed 1971) and Eagles Souths United Senior Soccer Club.

Eagles South United had earlier been formed by the amalgamation of the Townsville based clubs Polonia Eagles and Souths.
