Final season tables -- West Moreton British Football Association 1935

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

No final series were held this season.

A new association, known as the Brisbane and Districts Football Association was formed in late 1935, and administered a unified competition between the Brisbane and Ipswich clubs in 1937.

The Brisbane District Football Association and the West Moreton British Football Association were dissolved upon the formation of the new association.
