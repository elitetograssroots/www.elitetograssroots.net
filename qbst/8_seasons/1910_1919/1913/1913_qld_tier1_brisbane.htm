Note 1: Abbreviations in the results for matches where the result is known, but the score is unavailable: W = Won; D = Drew; L = Lost.

Note 2: Round 3 (17/05/1913) Bulimba Rangers vs. Corinthias was abandoned 3 minutes from time with the score 0-0, due to encroaching spectators. The match was replayed in full on 23/08/1913 and the result meant Bulimba Rangers won premiership by one point from Wellington.

Note 3: Results for six matches have been inferred from the points table published in the Brisbane Telegraph - 1 September 1913.

Note 4: Date for Round 6 matches interpreted from articles appearing in The Referee on 20/08/1913 and 27/08/1913.
