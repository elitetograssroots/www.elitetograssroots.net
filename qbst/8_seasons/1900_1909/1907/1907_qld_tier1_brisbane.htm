Final season tables -- Queensland British Football Association 1907

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

No final series were held this season. There was only one division of senior football this season.

There was very little reporting of the 1907 QBFA season in the contemporary press.

However, it is reasonable to assume that most or all of the Brisbane clubs failed to complete the 1907 season, because only the Milton club was active in 1908.
