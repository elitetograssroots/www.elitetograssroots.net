Final season tables -- Nepean 2001 to 2024 -- Index

The list below sets out the premiership winners and Grand Final winners (where known) for every top flight football in the Nepean league since 2001.

The Nepean FA was founded in 1961 as the Nepean Junior Soccer Association and has since also been known as the Nepean District Soccer Association and the Nepean District Soccer Football Association.

Information on seasons prior to 2000 is currently unavailable.

Many thanks to Steve Boyd of Penrith Rovers FC for assisting with data relating seasons prior to 2015.

 

A ranking of clubs by premiership wins is listed below.
