Final season tables -- ACT State League 2020

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

The start of the 2020 season was delayed until July due to the COVID-19 pandemic, after which it commenced in a condensed format.

No tables were published for this season on the Capital Football's results website. These tables have been created by reconciling the results of fixtures that were played.

There were no final series conducted by the ACT State League for this season, with Capital Football stating it was 'focusing on maximising participation opportunities for all players'.
