AFL Sydney

Premier Division -- Season 2021

 

Season notes

On 16 August 2021, AFL Sydney announced the 2021 season was cancelled due a COVID-19 outbreak across Greater Sydney and an associated lockdown.

No premiers were declared.

Camden was relegated to Division One after this season.
