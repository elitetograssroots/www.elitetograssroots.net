Sydney AFL

First Grade -- Season 2001

 

Season notes

Sydney Redbacks was a development team for the Sydney Swans. It competed in the Sydney AFL competition in 2001 and 2002 only.

The team consisted of Sydney Swans listed players who were not playing for Port Melbourne or the Sydney Swans, and a number of talented young NSW/ACT players with the potential to play AFL in future years.
