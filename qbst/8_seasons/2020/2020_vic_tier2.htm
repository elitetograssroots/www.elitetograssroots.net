Final season tables -- Football Victoria 2020

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

The 2020 season was suspended as of March 18 due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Only the top tier NPL Victoria had started, five rounds being completed before the shutdown.

On 22 July 2020 Football Victoria announced that the 2020 NPL season was cancelled.
