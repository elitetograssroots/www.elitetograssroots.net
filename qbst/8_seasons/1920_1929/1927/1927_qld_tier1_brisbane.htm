Final season tables -- Queensland Football Association 1927

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

No final series were held this season.

The Brisbane and District Football Association was formed in late 1927 to administer senior football competitions in Brisbane and Ipswich.

The QFA continued in its over-arching role of administering Queensland football at a state level until mid-1932 when succeeded by the Queensland Soccer Council.
