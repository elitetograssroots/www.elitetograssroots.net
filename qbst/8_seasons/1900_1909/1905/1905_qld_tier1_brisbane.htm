Final season tables -- Queensland British Football Association 1905

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

The top two teams after the regular season competed for the Challenge Cup in a final match.

There was only one division of senior football this season. The Ipswich club did not compete for the Brisbane Challenge Cup in 1905.
