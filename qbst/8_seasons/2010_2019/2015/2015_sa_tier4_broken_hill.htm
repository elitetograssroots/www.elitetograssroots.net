Final season tables -- Broken Hill Soccer Association 2015

 

In 2015 there was a schism between the four clubs, with two clubs leaving to form the Silver City Football Association.

The split was mended after the end of the 2015 season, with all clubs returning to the BHSA for the 2016 season.
