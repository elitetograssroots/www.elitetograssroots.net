Note 1: Abbreviations in the results for matches where the result is known, but the score is unavailable: W = Won; D = Drew; L = Lost; U = No record of result or if the match was played.

Note 2: Scores have been inferred for four matches for which the results are known, from the final records of Bush Rats and Call Backs published in Qld Times 04/10/1892.

Note 3: Round 1 match 16/07/1892 ended with the score Whitwood A 2 Blackstone Rovers 5 (Qld Times 19/07/1892). The result must have been reversed and the match awarded to Whitwood A based on subsequent points tables (for example, Qld Times 04/08/1892).

Note 4: Round 8 match 03/09/1892 was abandoned atter 15 minutes because Silkstone Stars left the field owing to a dispute (Qld Times 06/09/1892). The match was subsequently awarded to Call Backs (Qld Times 20/09/1892).
