Final season tables -- Football Wagga Wagga 2020

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

The start of the 2020 season was delayed until July due to the COVID-19 pandemic, after which it commenced in a condensed format.

Final standings for all senior men's competitions are not available on the association's results website.

The final Pascoe Cup table was compiled from results posted to the association's Facebook site.

The final positions of teams in the Gardiner Cup, Blake Cup and Nankivell Cup have been deduced from final series results.
