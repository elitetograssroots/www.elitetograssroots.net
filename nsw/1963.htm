Prior to the season, teams were graded into two sections based on 1962 rankings.

For the draw, the top six teams played each other twice, the bottom five teams played each other twice, and the lower five met the top six only once.

Four-point byes were only awarded to clubs for byes in the Rounds 1 to 10 and Round 12.
