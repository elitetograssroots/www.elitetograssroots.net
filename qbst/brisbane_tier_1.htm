Notes:

The list above refers to the final premiership standings:

  • Finals series played irregularly between 1923 and 1932 have been factored in, as these decided the premier team.
  • Final series played every season since 1959 (except 2000) are not factored in, as these decided the champions, a separate honour.
  • The State League that operated between 1962 and 1964 only comprised clubs from the Brisbane and Ipswich regions, so it remained effectively a Brisbane--based competition and not a genuine state--wide league.

 

Reserves teams achieved top four rankings as follows:

  • Brisbane Lions (1983 and 1985)
  • Brisbane Strikers (2008).

 

The 1920 season was chosen at the start point because complete records exist back to this season. This may be expanded to earlier seasons after further research.
