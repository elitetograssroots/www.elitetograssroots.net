Notes:

Adams Park United was renamed Kingsridge in 1979, and absorbed the existing Kingsridge Senior SC in the process. For the 1979 season, Kingsridge wore the playing strip of Adams Park United and had its home ground at Adams Park, Kingston.

After the 2003 season, Kingsridge merged its senior men's team with Logan City Kings.
