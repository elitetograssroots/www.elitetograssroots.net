Note 1: Round 1 Bundamba Rangers vs. Blackstone Rovers played on 20/06/1908 was abandoned with 12 minutes to play due to darkness with the score 2-2 (Brisbane Courier 22/06/1908). The final 12 minutes were played on 08/08/1908 with no score added (Qld Times 11/08/1908).

Note 2: The record of Dinmore Bush Rats in The Referee (Sydney) 09/09/1910 indicates that its Round 1 and 4 matches against Silkstone Montes were forfeited.

Note 3: Round 4 Blackstone Rovers vs. Bundamba Rangers was first played on 11/07/1908 at Blackstone, the result: Blackstone Rovers 2 Bundamba Rangers 5 (Qld Times 14/07/1908). The match was ordered to be replayed, the rematch occurring on 15/08/1908 (Qld Times 18/08/1908).
