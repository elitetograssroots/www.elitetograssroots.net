Note about the dates in the above tables:

* The Founded year is (usually) the year accepted by club sources.

** If an amalgamation has occurred, the merger date is usually used.

* The From year is the earliest season I have found the club in tables or list of clubs relating to a particular season.

** If a merger has occurred, the first season of the merged club is used.

** The derivative clubs contributing to the merger will have their records shown on the current club's page.

* The To year will be the current season for current senior clubs, or the most recent season competed at senior level in Brisbane/Qld State competition for past clubs.
