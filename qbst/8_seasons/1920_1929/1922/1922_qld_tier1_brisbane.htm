Note 1: After the first round of fixtures was completed on 05-07-1922, the QFA split Division 2 into two sections. Contemporary newspaper reports give conflicting reports on how the premier team was to be decided.

The QFA finally determined in late September that the leading teams from the two sections would play-off.

Bundamba Rangers refused to participate in the play-off match, believing that the team with the most points should be premiers. The Bundamba Rangers view is set out in a letter to the editor (Daily Mail 16 October 1922).

Note 2: There are missing results for most Round 18 matches and several postponed fixtures played in September 1922, due to incomplete press coverage.

 

Division 2 Premiership Play-off: Dinmore Wanderers awarded victory after Bundamba Rangers refused to play
