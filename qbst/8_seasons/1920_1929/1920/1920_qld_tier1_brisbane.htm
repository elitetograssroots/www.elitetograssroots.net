Final season tables -- Queensland Football Association 1920

The governing body changed its name from the Queensland British Football Association (QBFA) to the QFA this season. The organisation was still sometimes referred to as the QBFA in the media as late as 1921.

The Brisbane and Ipswich teams joined in a unified competition in 1921.

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

No final series were held this season. There was only one division of senior football this season.
