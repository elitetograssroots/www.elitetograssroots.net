Final season tables -- West Moreton British Football Association 1912

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

No final series were held this season.

For this season, three Ipswich clubs competed in the senior QBFA Challenge Cup. Therefore, the WMBFA was deemed to be a junior competition in 1912.

During this era, junior competition was not age limited, but instead was an indicator of status that delineated it from senior competition.

After this season the Ipswich clubs did not compete in the QBFA Challenge Cup and joined the newly formed West Moreton senior competition in 1913.

The Brisbane and Ipswich clubs next competed in a unified league in 1921.
