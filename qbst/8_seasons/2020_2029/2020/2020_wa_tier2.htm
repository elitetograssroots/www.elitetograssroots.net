Final season tables -- Football West 2020

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

The 2020 season was suspended in March due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Earlier results were annulled and the season recommenced in July in a condensed format.

In the condensed format, each division completed a round-robin Stage One.

In First Grade, Teams 1 to 6 progressed to the Upper Group; Teams 7 to 12 competed in the Lower Group. The top three teams in the Upper Group and the top team in the Lower Group qualified for the final series.

In Reserve Grade, teams competed a further five rounds but remained in one group. The top four teams qualified for the final series.

Promotion/relegation was suspended for this season.
