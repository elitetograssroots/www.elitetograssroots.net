Sydney Football League

First Grade -- Season 1985

 

Season notes

The final ladder below is based on a reconciliation of the regular season results.

This reconciled ladder does not match the official ladder first published in the Record 8-9-1985, page 13 which is mathematically incorrect because the points score for and against do not balance.

The official ladder containing these discrepancies was subsequently reproduced in the early 1986 editions of the Record and fails to include the deferred Round 8 game between Holroyd-Parramatta and Bankstown Sports (for example: Record 13-4-1986).
