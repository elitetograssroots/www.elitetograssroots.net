Final season tables -- Brisbane District Football Association 1934

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

No final series were held this season.

A new association, the Brisbane District Football Association was formed in late 1933, the successor to Football (Association Code) Limited in the role of administering senior competition in Brisbane.
