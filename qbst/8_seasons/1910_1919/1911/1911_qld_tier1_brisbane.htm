Note 1: Abbreviations in the results for matches where the result is known, but the score is unavailable: W = Won; D = Drew; L = Lost; U = No record of result or if the match was played.

Note 2: Round 11 Dinmore Bush Rats vs. Bulimba Rangers. Bulimba Rangers won when Dinmore forfeited the disputed match (Qld Times 12/08/1911), which meant Bulimba were premiers.

The original match played on 15/07/1911 was abandoned 20 minutes from time with the score 2-2 after (1) a collision between players (Qld Times 19/07/1911) or (2) after a Bush Rats player was sent off and refused to go (Qld Times 22/07/1911).

Note 3: Round 14 Bulimba Rangers vs. Valley. Result inferred, as Qld Times 12/08/1911 states Bulimba Rangers won the premiership after receiving the Dinmore Bush Rats forfeit. There is also a slight possibility this game could have been a draw.
