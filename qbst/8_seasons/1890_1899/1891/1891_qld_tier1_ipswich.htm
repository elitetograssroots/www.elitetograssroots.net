Final season tables -- West Moreton British Football Association 1891

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

The West Moreton British Football Association was formed in mid-August 1891 (Qld Times 17/08/1891).

The new association arranged a Challenge Cup competition commencing on 22 August 1891 and conducted on a knock-out format.
