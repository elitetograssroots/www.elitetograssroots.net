Final season tables -- Sport University of New England Football League 2020

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

Before the season began, the clubs based in Armidale and nearby surrounding districts disaffiliated from Northern NSW Football and competed in the Sport UNE Football League in 2020.

Therefore, these clubs did not compete in the Northern Inland Football 2020 season.
