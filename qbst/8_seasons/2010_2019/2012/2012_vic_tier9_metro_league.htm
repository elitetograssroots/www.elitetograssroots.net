Final season tables -- Football Federation Victoria 2012 -- Metropolitan League

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

No final series were conducted in FFV Metropolitan League for this season.

All teams in the three regional divisions of the Metropolitan League joined the new State League 5 in 2013.

The Metropolitan League continued in 2013, but was made up of teams competing in the Thirds competitions in 2012.
