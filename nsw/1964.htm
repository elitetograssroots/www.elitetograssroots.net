New South Wales Australian National Football League

Season 1964

 

Season notes

Southern Districts joined the league this season and was formed when Bankstown/Liverpool and Parramatta merged.

St. George lost their first two games of the season, then won their next 18 games (16 regular + 2 finals) to win the premiership.

South Sydney won their first two games of the season, then lost their next 16 games to win the Wooden Spoon.
