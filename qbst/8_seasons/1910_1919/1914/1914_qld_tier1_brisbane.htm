Note 1: Abbreviations in the results: W = Won; D = Drew; L = Lost; P = Postponed (and no record of the game later being played).

Note 2: Bulimba Rangers defeated Wellington in Round 1, however a protest by Wellington was upheld by the association and the game ordered to be replayed. By mid-July the game had not been replayed (The Referee 13 & 27 May 1914).

Note 3: All teams gained one point in Round 6. It is most likely that three draws were played. However, it is also possible the games were washed out and the Association awarded a point to each team.

Note 4: Results for five matches have been inferred from the points table published in The Referee (Sydney) - 22 July 1914.
