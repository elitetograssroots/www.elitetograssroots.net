Final season tables -- Queensland British Football Association 1909

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

No final series were held this season. There was only one division of senior football this season.

No Ipswich based teams participated in the 1909 Challenge Cup, however Dinmore Bush Rats competed in the 1909 Charity Cup competition and the 1910 Challenge Cup.

Note 1: Abbreviations in the results for matches where the result is known, but the score is unavailable: W = Won; D = Drew; L = Lost; U = No record of result or if the match was played.

Note 2: Results for Wellington's matches in Rounds 3, 4, 5, 9 and 10 have been inferred because there are no sources, and these results would see the points table leading at Wellington 13 Eskgrove 12 prior to the decisive Round 8 match on 31/07/1909.

Note 3: If Wellington and Eskgrove finished equal on points after the regular season and their Round 8 match was a play-off decider, then the game could not have ended in a draw. Therefore, the best interpretation in the lack of other evidence is that this was a regular season match.
