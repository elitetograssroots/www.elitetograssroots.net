Final season tables -- Central Coast Football 2024

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

Points per game was used to rank teams in divisions in which all teams did not play the same number of games.

Promotion and relegation between the Premier League and Division 1 was based on club championship standings, calculated across the three grades.

For this season, the team finishing first after the regular season are designated 'champions', and the grand final winner are 'premiers' (the opposite of most leagues in Australia).
