In 1962 there was a split between the Queensland Soccer Football Association and the newly founded Queensland Soccer Federation.

It is possible Bulimba Rangers continued in 1962 with the Association (list of Association teams for that season currently unknown).

By 1966 Bulimba Rangers was either absorbed by another club or was defunct.
