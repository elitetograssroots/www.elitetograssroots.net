Final season tables -- Brisbane and Ipswich Soccer Football Association 1962

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

The Queensland Soccer Federation (QSF) was formed this season by clubs that broke away from the Queensland Soccer Football Association (QSFA).

Of the ten clubs competing in Division 1 in 1961, only Annerley and YMCA (renamed Triangles) remained with the Association in 1962.
