Note 1: Abbreviations in the results for matches where the result is known, but the score is unavailable: W = Won; D = Drew; L = Lost.

Note 2: Round 2 Bundamba Rangers vs. Ipswich City 17/05/1913 was abandoned in second half with Bundamba Rangers leading 2-0, after three Ipswich City players (and one Bundamba player) were sent off, and the Ipswich City team walked off. The match was ordered to be replayed in full on 31/05/1913.

Note 3: Round 7 Blackstone Rovers vs. St Stephens. St Stephens failed to appear at kick-off on 02/08/1913. The game was ordered to be replayed on 16/08/1913, but no result is available for this match.

Note 4: Bundamba Rangers had won the premiership after the Round 9 matches were played. Therefore, Blackstone Rovers must have failed to win at least one of their games for which the result is unknown (Rounds 7 and 9).
