Final season tables -- Queensland British Football Association 1912

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

No final series were held this season. There was only one division of senior football this season.

After this season the Ipswich clubs did not compete in the QBFA Challenge Cup and joined the newly formed West Moreton senior competition in 1913.

The Brisbane and Ipswich clubs next competed in a unified league in 1921.

Note 1: Abbreviations in the results for matches where the result is known, but the score is unavailable: W = Won; D = Drew; L = Lost; XXX = No record of result or if the match was played.

Note 2: Round 1 Valley vs. Eskgrove result inferred as neither team was listed as a winner in Referee 15/05/1912 and Eskgroves had 3 points up to Round 3 (Referee 29/05/1912).

Note 3: Round 16 Blackstone Rovers vs. Bulimba Rangers was the decisive match that determined the premiership.

Note 4: Round 18 Eskgrove vs. Bulimba Rangers result inferred. 'Bulimba lost to Blackstone by a very narrow margin' (Brisbane Courier 19/10/1912). Bringing the match date forward to 17 August 1912 matches reports that Blackstone had a game in hand at Rounds 17 & 18 (Qld Times 21/09/1912 & Referee 25/09/1912)
