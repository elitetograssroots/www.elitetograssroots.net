Final season tables -- Ipswich and West Moreton British Football Association 1904

The Ipswich and WBMFA was in recess this season.

It was reported that the Ipswich and WMBFA was to be reformed (Qld Times 31/05/1904), but no reports of local competitions appeared subsequently.

However, four Ipswich based teams competed this season with the Brisbane clubs for this season's QBFA Challenge Cup and Charity Cup.

 

Ipswich and West Moreton British FA - Senior Premiership -- 1904
