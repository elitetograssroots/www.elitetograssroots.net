Final season tables -- West Moreton British Football Association 1894

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

After this season, the West Moreton British Football Association was in recess, until a successor body (the Ipswich and WMBFA) was established in 1899.

Note 1: Blackstone Rovers and Ipswich FC merged to form Ipswich Rovers in 1895.

Note 2: After this season Bush Rats changed its home ground from the New Chum Colliery Ground to Nunn's Paddock, Dinmore. The latter was the home ground of Dinmore FC which folded after this season, with several players and officials moving to Bush Rats.

The club became Dinmore Bush Rats from the 1895 season onwards, although it was still regularly still referred to as 'Bush Rats' in local reporting.

 

Premiership play-off: Bush Rats 3 Blackstone Rovers 1
