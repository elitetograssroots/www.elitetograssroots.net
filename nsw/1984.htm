Sydney Football League

First Grade -- Season 1984

 

Season notes

The final ladder below is based on a reconciliation of the regular season results.

This reconciled ladder does not match the official ladder (Refer: Record 14-4-1985, page 13) which is mathematically incorrect because the points score for and against do not balance.

 

Ashbury Oval was renamed WH Wagener Oval after this season.

Football Park, Macquarie Fields was renamed Edelsten Park during this season.
