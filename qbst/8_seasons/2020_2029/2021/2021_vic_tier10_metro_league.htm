First-grade teams are listed in bold.

The 2021 season was suspended in early August due a State Government lockdown.

On 3 September 2021 Football Victoria announced its decision to cancel all metropolitan Melbourne competitions.

No premiers or champions were declared. Promotion/Relegation was suspended for this season.
