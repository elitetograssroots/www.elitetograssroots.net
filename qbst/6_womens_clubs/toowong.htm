Brisbane Women's Division 1

 

New World United was founded in 1973. It changed its name to Dutton Park United in 1978.

The club merged with Toowong and became Riverside United in 1981. The club's home ground was T. J. Doyle Memorial Drive, Dutton Park.

Toowong demerged from Riverside United after two seasons.
