New South Wales Australian National Football League

Division One -- First Grade -- Season 1973

 

Season notes

The final series was increased to the top five teams for this season. A top five final series was used from 1973 until 1978.

After the first 7 rounds, for Rounds 8-10 there was a 'mini round' of 3 matches (top 4 played each other, bottom 4 played each other), then the second 7 rounds were played.
