Final season tables -- Canterbury District Soccer Football Association 2020

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

The 2020 season was suspended in March due to the COVID-19 pandemic. The season recommenced in July in a condensed format.

No final series were conducted by the Canterbury DSFA for this season, except for the Bill Brackenbury Cup.
