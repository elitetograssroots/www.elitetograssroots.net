Notes:

* Latrobe - Merton Rovers unregistered player

Merton Rovers played an unregistered player in their 2-1 win against Latrobe on 29 June 1935 (Round 9).

The BDFA awarded Latrobe two points and deducted two points from Merton Rovers.

 

* Wynnum - Toowong unregistered player and relegation saga

At a BDFA executive meeting on 9 September 1935 it was decided Wynnum had played an unregistered player in Rounds 13 and 14.

Consequently, Toombul United received a point and Wynnum had a point deducted for a 3-3 draw Round 13.

The result of the Round 14 match Toowong 0 Wynnum 5 was annulled and the game ordered to be replayed.

The replayed game was scheduled for Raymond Park on 21 September 1935, but Toowong’s ball burst during practice and Wynnum failed to bring a ball.

A further attempt to complete the game was scheduled for Lang Park on 28 September 1935. Toowong failed to appear, forfeited the game and was relegated.

Toowong was listed to appear in Division 3 in 1936, but there were not enough clubs to support a division so Toowong sat the 1936 season out.
