Gold Coast United was resurrected as a club when it was formed upon the merger of Gold Coast Athletic and Gold Coast Galaxy in August 2017.

The Athletic and Galaxy clubs had themselves only recently been formed and were to join the Football Queensland Premier League in 2018.
