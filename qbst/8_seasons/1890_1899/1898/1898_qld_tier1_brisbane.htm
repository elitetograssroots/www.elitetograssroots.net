Final season tables -- Queensland British Football Association 1898

First-grade teams are listed in bold.

No final series were held this season. There was only one division of senior football this season.

The Ipswich and West Moreton British Football Association was formed on 20/02/1899 (Qld Times 21/02/1899).

The leading Ipswich clubs continued to participate in the Brisbane Challenge Cup in 1899 and following seasons, as well as for the local Ipswich and West Moreton premiership.

Note 1: Abbreviations in the results for matches where the result is known, but the score is unavailable: W = Won; D = Drew; L = Lost; U = No record of result or if the match was played.

Note 2: Round 5 matches were originally scheduled for 04/06/1898. Bundamba Rangers vs. Dinmore Bush Rats was cancelled when the appointed Brisbane referee failed to appear (Qld Times 02/06/1898). Rosebank vs. Blackstone Rovers was probably postponed due to wet weather in Brisbane on 04/06/1898.

Note 3: For the Round 6 match Bundamba Rangers vs. Norman, the venue was switched from Bundamba to Brisbane at the request of Norman. Bundamba Rangers agreed, rather than accept a forfeit from Norman due to the lack of its players willing to travel to Ipswich (Qld Times 25/08/1898).

Note 4: Round 8 match: Norman 3 Blackstone Rovers 2. Blackstone Rovers lodged a protest that Norman had played two unregistered players (Qld Times 06/09/1898).

The protest must have been successful and the result over-turned because a play-off match was required. The result of the protest had not been determined as at Qld Times 15/09/1898, but a play-off was scheduled as reported in Qld Times 22/09/1898.
